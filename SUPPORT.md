# Support

`agent-skills` is a solo-maintained, public-good open-source project. It is not a commercial product
and does not come with a support channel or response commitment.

- **No SLA.** Issues may remain open for an extended time. PRs may not be reviewed, or may be closed
  without detailed feedback if they do not fit the project's scope.
- **No private support.** Please do not email the maintainer for help with a specific repository,
  ticket, pipeline, or internal workflow.
- **Security issues** are the exception. See [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for the reporting process.

## If you have a real bug

GitHub Issues are open. Include the smallest reproducer you can, the skill name, the agent (Claude
Code, Cursor, etc.), and relevant configuration details with secrets stripped.

## If something doesn't fit your workflow

Forking is welcome under the MIT license. A custom fork tuned to your stack, tools, or governance
model may be the right path when your needs diverge from this repository's intentionally small
upstream scope.
